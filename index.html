Meet the Artist

Slothie Studios was founded in Summer 2016 when I took place in the Summer Company buisness program. It started as merchandise for a Virtual Pet SIM, but I quickly found a passion and talent for making plushies.

Over the years I've expanded my skills and equipment to be able to sell embroidered plushies and goods, including patches. I've also been producing enamel pins.
